1. Introduction

The "Odia Bhasa" app respects the intellectual property rights of others. In accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA), if you believe that any of your copyrighted material has been used in our app without your permission, you can notify us by following the procedure below.

2. Reporting Copyright Infringement

We take complaints of copyright infringement seriously.

If you are a copyright owner or their agent, and you believe that any content in our app infringes your copyright, please send us a written notice.

3. Notice Requirements

To be a valid DMCA Notice, it must contain the following information:

  • Signature: Electronic or physical signature of the copyright owner or authorized person.
  • Description of Work: A clear description of the copyrighted work that you claim has been infringed.
  • Location of Material: A clear description of where the infringing material is located in the app (e.g., Screenshot/Screen Recording or Name).
  • Contact Info: Your address, telephone number, and email address.
  • Good Faith Statement: A statement that you have a good faith belief that use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner or the law.
  • Affidavit: A statement that the information provided is accurate and that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.

4. Our Action

Upon receipt of a valid DMCA Notice, we will review the notice and take appropriate action in accordance with applicable law. If we remove any content, we may also notify the person who provided that content about this.

Our goal is to provide a safe and legal educational platform for children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our DMCA Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
