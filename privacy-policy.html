1. Introduction

Thank you for using the "Odia Bhasa" app. This app is designed to help children in Odisha learn Odia letters, numbers, and practice via a digital slate. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

2. Target Audience (Children)

This application is primarily directed at children (Ages 3-12) and their parents. Because our users include children, we follow strict guidelines to ensure a safe environment.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

We do NOT collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII).

We do not ask for or collect:

  • Names, Email Addresses, or Phone Numbers.
  • Physical Addresses or precise GPS Location.
  • Device microphone or camera data for recording purposes.

4. Data Collection & Safety

No Login Required: You can use the entire app without creating an account. There is no Login or Sign-up feature.

No Marketing Push Notifications: Our app does not send any marketing or spam push notifications.

Our app is completely ad-free and will remain so forever.

5. Permissions Used

The app requests minimal permissions necessary for functionality, specifically to access online content.

Permission What It Allows
INTERNET Allows the app to fetch online content, such as books and this privacy policy page.

6. Third-Party Services & Parental Gate

The app may contain links to the developer's other apps, Instagram, or YouTube channel.

Parental Gate Protection: To prevent children from accidentally leaving the app, all external links are protected by a "Math Challenge" (e.g., 10 + 5 = ?).

Only users who can solve the math problem (Parents/Guardians) can access these outside links.

7. Content & Copyright

Chhabila Madhu Barnabodha: Used for educational purposes. Original Poems: Most content is written by the developer. We do not sell books. Contact us for copyright concerns.

8. Children’s Privacy

We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the Google Play Families Policy.

Our app is designed for children and does not collect, store, or share personal information.

We do not display advertisements and do not use the Android Advertising ID (AAID).

Any error or crash information is generated locally on the user’s device and is not sent to us or any external server.

Any data used by the app is limited to what is necessary for basic functionality and is not used for tracking or profiling.

9. Government Disclaimer

This app is not affiliated with the Government of India or Odisha. The National Anthem is included solely for educational purposes.

10. Parents’ Rights & Controls

Since we do not collect data, there is no data to delete. However, parents can contact us anytime for questions.

Feedback Support: We provide support in Odia and English. If you email us, we will reply as soon as possible (ASAP).

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
