1. Introduction and Purpose

Thank you for using the "Odia Bhasa" app. This app is specifically designed to provide a safe platform for children in Odisha to learn Odia letters (vowels, consonants), combined letters, numbers, and practice via a digital slate.

We respect your and your children's privacy.

2. Data Collection and Child Safety (COPPA/GDPR-K)

"We do not collect any personal data such as name, email, address, or location from children."

COPPA / GDPR-K Compliance: Our app fully complies with the 'Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)' and child safety regulations. We prioritize children's safety:

  • No Login Required: No account creation or sign-up is needed to use the app.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect Personally Identifiable Information (PII) from users.
  • Location: We do not track your geographic location.

3. Ads (AdMob) and Settings

We use "Google AdMob" services to maintain the app. However, we have taken strict measures for children's safety:

We use Google AdMob, but we have configured it to show only "Child-Directed" and "Non-Personalized" ads.
  • This means we do not show Targeted Ads based on user data.
  • Inappropriate ads for children will not be shown. It is Family Friendly.

4. Content and Copyright

Chhabila Madhu Barnabodha: We have included the famous book 'Chhabila Madhu Barnabodha' in the app for Odia learning. It is used for educational purposes only. If you are the owner of this book and wish to remove it from our app, please email us immediately.

Other Books: Most poems and stories in the app are written by the creator of the "Odia Bhasa" app. If you wish to add your child-friendly book to our app, you can mail us. We do not sell any books.

5. Government Disclaimer

This app is not affiliated with the Government of India or the Government of Odisha.

The National Anthem and State Anthem included in this app are for educational purposes only to teach children. It is not an official government app.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
