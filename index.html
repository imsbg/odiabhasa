📚 ବର୍ଣ୍ଣମାଳା ଶିକ୍ଷା:

  1. ସ୍ୱରବର୍ଣ୍ଣ ଓ ବ୍ୟଞ୍ଜନବର୍ଣ୍ଣ: ଅ ରୁ କ୍ଷ ପର୍ଯ୍ୟନ୍ତ ପ୍ରତ୍ୟେକ ଅକ୍ଷରକୁ ଶବ୍ଦ ଏବଂ ବାକ୍ୟ ସହ ଶିଖନ୍ତୁ ।

  3. ଯୁକ୍ତାକ୍ଷର: କଠିନ ଯୁକ୍ତାକ୍ଷର ଗୁଡ଼ିକୁ (ଯେପରି- ଙ୍କ, ଞ୍ଚ, ଷ୍ଟ) ସହଜ ଉଦାହରଣ ସହ ବୁଝନ୍ତୁ ।

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Odia Bhasa app?

Odia Bhasa is a fully Odia language learning app designed especially for children and beginners. The app helps users learn Odia alphabets, numbers, writing, stories, prayers, and cultural content in a simple and engaging way.

Is Odia Bhasa completely in Odia language?

Yes ✅
The app is 100% in Odia language, making it ideal for kids, parents, and anyone who wants to learn Odia in its pure form.

What is the mission behind the Odia Bhasa app?

The main mission is to provide free, high-quality digital education and preserve the Odia language. As the creator Sandeep Biswal G says, "Learning a language especially one's mother tongue should not depend on subscriptions or ads." Therefore, the app is built completely free and safe for everyone.

Is the app free? Are there any ads?

Yes, the app is completely free and 100% ad-free 🚫📢
There are no advertisements, ensuring a safe and distraction-free learning experience for children.

Which app is best for Odisha Board (BSE) school books from Class 1 to 10?

Odia Bhasa app is the best free app to read all Odisha Board (BSE) school books from Class 1 to 10. It includes subjects like Odia Sahitya, Math, Science, History, and Geography directly inside the app, formatted perfectly for mobile reading.

Who developed the Odia Bhasa app?

The app is developed by Sandeep Biswal G, with the aim of promoting Odia language, culture, and education digitally.

What can kids learn from this app?

Children can learn:

  • Swarabarna (ସ୍ୱରବର୍ଣ୍ଣ) with voice pronunciation
  • Byanjanabarna (ବ୍ୟଞ୍ଜନବର୍ଣ୍ଣ) with voice pronunciation
  • Juktakhyara (ଯୁକ୍ତାକ୍ଷର)
  • Phala (ଫଳା)
  • Odia Sankhya (ଓଡ଼ିଆ ସଂଖ୍ୟା)
  • Chhabila Madhu Barnabodha
Does the app have writing practice?

Yes ✍️ The app includes writing practice / digital slate, where kids can practice writing Odia letters just like writing on a slate.

Are Odia story books available in the app?

Yes 📚 The app contains many Odia story books suitable for children, helping them improve reading skills and moral learning.

Does the app include school prayers and songs?

Yes 🙏🎶 The app includes:

  • Odia school prarthana
  • National Anthem
  • Odisha State Song / Anthem
Is there an Odia calendar in the app?

Yes 📅 The app has a detailed Odia Calendar, which shows:

  • Odia month names
  • Seasons (Rutu / ଋତୁ)
  • All important festivals and holidays
Is this app suitable for small children?

Absolutely 👍 The app is kid-friendly, easy to use, ad-free, and designed especially for early learners and school-going children.

Where can I download the Odia Bhasa app?

You can download the app only from the Google Play Store

Does the app require internet?

Most learning content works offline, so children can learn anytime, anywhere.

Why should I choose Odia Bhasa app?

Because it is:

  • 100% Odia
  • Ad-free & safe for kids
  • Rich in language, culture, and education
  • Designed with love for Odia language ❤️
