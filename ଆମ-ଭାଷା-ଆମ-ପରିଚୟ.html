ସାଧାରଣ ପ୍ରଶ୍ନ (FAQs)

1. Is the Odia Bhasa app really free?

Yes! The app is 100% free forever. There are no premium subscriptions, no paywalls, and absolutely no ads. Learning the mother tongue should be accessible to everyone.

2. What age group is this app for?

It is primarily designed for kids and toddlers who are just starting to learn the Odia language. However, anyone who wants to learn the basics of reading and writing Odia can use it.

3. What exactly can my child learn?

Your child can learn the complete Odia Alphabet (Swara Varna and Vyanjana Varna), practice tracing and writing letters, and learn how to read basic words. It is an all-in-one foundation app.

4. Does the app require an internet connection?

Once downloaded from the Google Play Store, the app functions offline so your child can learn anywhere, anytime without needing an active internet connection.

5. Who developed this app?

The app is developed with love for the Odia language by Sandeep Biswal G.
